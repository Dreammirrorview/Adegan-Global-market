## Platform Overview

Adegan Global Market is a comprehensive e-commerce platform that connects buyers and sellers across Nigeria. Our platform features:

- **Product Marketplace**: Browse products across multiple categories
- **Integrated Banking**: Secure payment processing and account management
- **Seller Dashboard**: Easy product upload and management
- **Admin Dashboard**: Complete system control and monitoring

## For Buyers

### How to Browse Products

1. **Visit Product Wall**
   - Navigate to `index.html`
   - Products are displayed in a grid layout

2. **Filter by Category**
   - Click on category buttons (Food, Electronics, etc.)
   - View filtered product results

3. **View Product Details**
   - Click on any product to see details
   - View price, seller information, and description

### How to Place an Order

1. **Click "Place Order"** on a product card

2. **Fill Order Details**
   - Quantity required
   - Size (if applicable)
   - Color preference
   - Your name and phone
   - Delivery address

3. **Submit Order**
   - Click "Place Order" button
   - You'll receive confirmation

4. **Contact Seller**
   - Use seller's email/phone to negotiate
   - Arrange payment and delivery

### Payment Methods

- **Direct Transfer**: Transfer to seller's account
- **Bank Payment**: Through platform payment system
- **Cash on Delivery**: If seller offers this option

### Tips for Buyers

- ✅ Verify seller ratings and reviews
- ✅ Compare prices across sellers
- ✅ Negotiate directly with sellers
- ✅ Check product descriptions carefully
- ✅ Use secure payment methods

### Customer Management

#### Create New Customer Account
1. Go to "Create New Customer Account" section
2. Fill customer details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Address
3. Click "Create Account"
4. Account number is automatically generated
5. Share credentials with customer

#### View Customer Details
- Browse customer table
- Click "View" button on any customer
- See detailed customer information

#### Close Customer Account
- Click "Close" button on customer table
- Confirm closure action
- Account is permanently removed

### Transaction Management

#### Credit Customer Account
1. Select customer from dropdown
2. Enter amount
3. Add description
4. Click "Credit Account"
5. Customer receives credit

#### Debit Customer Account
1. Select customer from dropdown
2. Enter amount
3. Add description
4. Click "Debit Account"
5. Customer balance is reduced

#### Transfer to External Bank
1. Enter recipient details:
   - Recipient Name
   - Recipient Account Number
   - Bank Name
   - Amount
   - Purpose
2. Click "Transfer Funds"
3. Transfer processed via NIBSS

## Payment System

### Account Structure

- **Admin Account**: 0000000000 (Main system account)
- **Customer Accounts**: 10-digit unique numbers
- **Serial Numbers**: For identification purposes

### Transaction Types

#### Credit Transaction
- Money added to customer account
- Can be done by admin
- Includes: Sales proceeds, refunds, deposits

#### Debit Transaction
- Money removed from customer account
- Can be done by admin
- Includes: Purchases, fees, withdrawals

#### External Transfer
- Transfer to other bank accounts
- Processed via NIBSS
- Instant settlement
- ₦50 fee per transfer

### Payment Methods Supported

1. **Bank Transfer**
   - Direct account-to-account transfer
   - Instant processing
   - ₦50 fee

2. **Card Payment**
   - Visa/MasterCard
   - 1.5% + ₦100 fee
   - Instant processing

3. **USSD Payment**
   - Mobile banking
   - ₦100 fee
   - Instant processing

4. **Wallet Transfer**
   - Internal transfers
   - Free
   - Instant processing

### Security Features

- Two-factor authentication
- BVN verification
- Transaction monitoring
- Fraud detection
- Secure encryption
